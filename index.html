Manifesto

The bourgeoisie has torn away from the family its sentimental veil, and has reduced the family relation to a mere money relation. That is why Thrash Deposit has come for your face. Every album is a fist, every fist a conflict, wherein the proletariat deposits back into the gears of oppression its own fruits of profound alienation.
